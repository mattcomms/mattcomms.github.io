How I started

If you told me I was going to be drawing on a daily basis prior to 2015.
I would have laughed in your face...

- Oct 27th, 2015 -

On this date I posted a very simple Hellboy design built off basic shapes.
After receiving some very positive words of encouragement I decided to pursue illustration futher. My drawings have changed dramatically over these few short years and I am extremely grateful for the push in the right direction.
