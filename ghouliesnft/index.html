The specs

Each Ghoulie is unique, randomly generated from over 50,000 possible outcomes weighted by rarity. Traits include skulls, glasses, hats, clothing, jewellery, backgrounds and more!

The Ghoulies are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain.
